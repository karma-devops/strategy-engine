14. Page Architecture (SOLID/DDD)
One page per responsibility. SPA hash router. No tab spaghetti.

Routes

#/ — Landing (public)
#/about — About (public)
#/faq — FAQ (public)
#/login — Login (public)
#/dashboard — Equity curve, open positions, fleet summary, activity
#/engines — Fleet management, add/edit/remove engines
#/engine/:slug — Single engine: signals, trades, position, settings
#/trades — Trade history (full-width table, filtering, export)
#/backtests — Run backtests, compare results, equity curves
#/monitoring — Scores, rotation, alerts
#/settings — Account, credentials, risk parameters
#/withdrawals — Withdrawal management
#/assistant — Full-page AI chat (deep analysis, code, strategy review)

Full Page (/assistant)

Deep analysis
engine-1 context 30d backtest + attach
Should I switch FARTCOIN from 15m to 30m? Show me the backtest comparison.
FARTCOIN v1.3: 15m vs 30m (90d, $100)
15m: 420 trades, +367.56%, 90.2% WR, PF 5.10, DD 15.46%
30m: 120 trades, +112.43%, 95.8% WR, PF 13.26, DD 2.17%
30m has higher win rate (95.8% vs 90.2%) and much lower drawdown (2.17% vs 15.46%), but 15m captures more profit through higher trade frequency. If risk-adjusted returns matter, 30m is the safer choice. If raw return is the goal, keep 15m.
